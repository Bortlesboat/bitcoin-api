# Satoshi API - First Testers Outreach Kit

Use this to get the first 10-20 real testers for `bitcoinsapi.com` fast.

Core rule: do not sell "a free Bitcoin API."
Sell one job:

**Know when to send Bitcoin and when to wait.**

That is concrete, easy to understand, and easy to test.

## Primary link

Send people to one of these, in order:

1. `https://bitcoinsapi.com/bitcoin-fee-api`
2. `https://bitcoinsapi.com/api/v1/fees/recommended`
3. `https://bitcoinsapi.com/bitcoin-api-for-ai-agents` for MCP-native audiences

## What to ask for

Ask for testing, not applause.

Use this ask:

> I want 5-10 people to try this and tell me what is missing, confusing, or broken.

## Who to contact first

Prioritize people who can actually use or amplify it:

1. Bitcoin wallet developers
2. Builders making bots, dashboards, or agents
3. Bitcoin newsletter writers and educators
4. Indie hackers who like free developer tools

## Site talking points

Repeat these everywhere:

- Free
- No signup for basic testing
- Open source
- Self-hostable
- Answers "send now or wait?"
- Works for apps and AI agents

## Do not lead with these

- "35 endpoints"
- generic "Bitcoin REST API"
- infrastructure details before the use case
- comparisons unless someone asks

## Success target for week one

Good target:

- 10 people try the endpoint
- 3 people reply with real feedback
- 1 person integrates or self-hosts it

That is enough to learn what to fix next.
